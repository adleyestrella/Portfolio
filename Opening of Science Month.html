What I have learned in this event is that how important science really is. With science, it helped us in in discover things that help us with innovation and without it, we wouldn't be here today. I can apply what I learned in science to real life in many different ways. If I learned about hygiene, I can practice it and stay healthy. Science can teach us in making good decision which we can apply this in real life when we have to make a big decision. In every Science month celebration, I wasn't active in this celebration but I did join it during the science quiz bee. Honestly, it was fun and exciting moment because it was first competition that I attend in high school. Even though I didn't win the contest but it was fun enough for me. If I were to teach science to a classmate, I would explain it in a simple way and use examples. I would explain how this topic help us solve problems in real-life. If I have prepared a presentation, I would use pictures to show what topic is about. It is important to celebrate the Science Month Celebration because it helps students become more interested and understand how science affect all of us. This opening makes students be prepared and have fun for the activities of the event. It also remind us that science isn't just a subject in school but a tool that we use to make our lives easier.
