Cluster Meet

Credits to Jade Imperial

The most important lesson I have learned in Cluster Meet is the value of sportsmanship and unity of each school teams. It reminds us that in every game, we should always play fair and give respect to our opponents. I can apply what I have learned to real-life situations by playing by the rules and show respect to our opponents and also my teammates. I did not participate in this event because I don't want to miss any class and I am not competitive. If I were to teach this topic to my classmates, I would explain the purpose of this event and it is a place where each school show their competitive spirit and talents. I would also explain to them that it is not about winning, it is about fun and doing your best. It is important to celebrate Cluster Meet because it shows how talented of each school when they all come together in one place.
