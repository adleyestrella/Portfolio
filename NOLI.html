What I have learned in this event is that this helps us understand the history, struggles, and social issues during the Spanish colonization in the Philippines. Watching the play reminds us the problems that our ancestors endured and it can also be seen in modern days. It also makes the story easier to understand and shows the message of José Rizal about injustice, nationalism, and the importance of fighting for freedom. The play shows how we appreciate our Filipino culture and literature deeply.
